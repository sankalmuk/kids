📖 Crossword Puzzle
How to play: Click any white cell to highlight a word. The selected word is shown in yellow.
Then click a word from the Word Bank above to fill it in automatically.
🔍 Word Search
How to play: Find the hidden words in the grid — they run horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
Click each letter of a word one by one, then press Check Answers to confirm your selection.
🔀 Jumbled Words
How to play: Each card shows a scrambled word and a hint. Click a card to select it (it turns yellow).
Then click the correct word from the Word Bank above to fill in the answer.
🔗 Match Pairs
How to play: Each word has been split into two halves. Click one item on the left, then its matching half on the right.
A correct pair turns green instantly. A wrong pair flashes red briefly.
✏️ Fill in the Blanks
How to play: Click a word from the Word Bank (shown above each story) to select it — it will highlight.
Then click a blank (____) in the story to place the word there.
❓ Quiz
How to play: Read the clue for each question and choose the best answer from the four options.
Press Check Answers to see your results. Correct answers turn green, wrong ones turn red.
🔤 Missing Letters
How to play: Each word has one or two letters missing (shown as _).
Type the missing letter(s) in the small box beside each word. Use the Word Bank as a hint for the full words.
🔒 Letter Coding
How to play: The table at the top shows a secret code — each letter of A–Z is replaced by a different letter.
Use the table to decode each coded word and type your answer in the box.
⭐ Choose the Best Answer
How to play: Read the description and choose the word it best describes from the four options given.
Press Check Answers when done — correct answers turn green, wrong ones turn red.
